# Cutie Helper Training & Promotion

This page outlines the training expectations, restrictions, and promotion path for <RoleBadge role="Cutie Helper" badgeIcon="cutie_helper_role_icon.png" color="#38c8e8" /> (Trainee Moderators).

## Restrictions

<Card title="⚠️ What Cutie Helpers Cannot Do" status="error">
  <p>Cutie Helpers do <strong>not</strong>:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Issue warnings</li>
    <li>Issue mutes or timeouts</li>
    <li>Issue bans</li>
    <li>Make final moderation decisions</li>
  </ul>
  <p>These tasks are for <RoleBadge role="Moderator" badgeIcon="moderator_role_icon.png" color="#e68027" /> only. If you encounter a situation requiring these actions, report it in <ChannelBadge label="📗cutie-helper" link="https://discord.com/channels/734595073920204940/1481566791569248286"/>.</p>
</Card>

## Training Expectations

All <RoleBadge role="Cutie Helper" badgeIcon="cutie_helper_role_icon.png" color="#38c8e8" /> are expected to complete the following during their training period:

<CardGrid columns={2}>
  <Card title="📋 Required Tasks" status="info">
    <ul>
      <li>Handle verification tickets on your own or with a Moderator</li>
      <li>Complete at least <strong>2 training/test tickets</strong></li>
      <li>Contribute to ongoing Mod discussions</li>
    </ul>
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="🎯 Expected Qualities" status="success">
    <ul>
      <li>Confidently handling verification tickets</li>
      <li>Good understanding of how server issues are handled</li>
      <li>Calm and respectful communication towards members and staff</li>
    </ul>
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

### Learning Opportunities

- **Shadow Moderators** to learn proper workflow and procedures
- **Observe tickets** to understand how issues are resolved
- **Ask questions** in <ChannelBadge label="📗cutie-helper" link="https://discord.com/channels/734595073920204940/1481566791569248286"/> — Moderators are expected to help train you!

:::tip Use Verification-Chat Correctly
<ChannelBadge label="💬verification-chat" link="https://discord.com/channels/734595073920204940/737215117049069610"/> is for **verification ticket discussions only**. All other questions, issues, and discussions should go to <ChannelBadge label="📗cutie-helper" link="https://discord.com/channels/734595073920204940/1481566791569248286"/>.

## Promotion to Moderator

### Requirements

To be promoted to <RoleBadge role="Moderator" badgeIcon="moderator_role_icon.png" color="#e68027" />, you must demonstrate:

- ✅ **Good judgment** — Making appropriate decisions in various situations
- ✅ **Consistent presence** — Being regularly active and available
- ✅ **Rule understanding** — Knowing and correctly applying server rules
- ✅ **Calm and respectful communication** — Professional interactions with everyone
- ✅ **Reliability and no drama involvement** — Dependable and drama-free

### Timeline

| Path | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Standard promotion | **2 months** |
| Exceptional progress | **1 month** |

### Process

Promotion is decided via **Moderator voting**. When the team believes you're ready, they will discuss and vote on your promotion.

:::note
Focus on doing your best work and demonstrating the qualities above. Promotions come naturally when you consistently show you're ready for more responsibility!
:::
